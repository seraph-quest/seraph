# 06. Presence And Reach

## Goal

Seraph should feel present outside the moment when the user opens chat.

## Layers

- ambient observation
- stateful timing and interruption logic
- proactive delivery inside the product
- external reach through notifications and channels
- native-feeling local surfaces

## Why This Matters

A guardian that only exists in an open browser tab is still mostly reactive.

## Research Questions

- what should count as interruption-worthy?
- which channels earn their place first?
- what is the right boundary between ambient presence and intrusive behavior?
- how should observer confidence affect delivery aggressiveness?
